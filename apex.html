The FSP Note Features

Principal protected via the FSP Digital Dollar (USDB)

  1. Sign up for your investment
  2. Transfer funds via USD ACH or via USDC/ USDT stablecoins
  3. Your investment is locked up for 2 years
  4. Receive FSP Digital Dollar (USDB)
  5. Return FSP Digital Dollar at the end of Term, deposit the digital dollar & receive units of the underlying and your deposit back. Alternatively, earn bonus units if you decide to roll up your deposits.

Portfolio pool growth is not guaranteed.

Trade FSP Digital Dollar via swaps to USDC or via the partner Earn program even during the fixed deposit term.
